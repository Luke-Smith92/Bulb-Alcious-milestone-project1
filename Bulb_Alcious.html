Welcome all bulb lovers to a family run website, dedicated in everything that grows from a bulb. Some of are favourites are spring Daffodiles, to the majestic summer lilies, and the deep blue hyacinth for autumn.
We supply a tiny amount of happyiness all year round when you see the flowers blooming.
Not only do we supply bulbs but also lovingly cut flowers for all types of occasions, from weddings to anniversarys and the day of love valentines day. Expertly picked or cut and delivered to your door.

Why choose us for bulbs or cut flowers!

All our bulbs and flowers are grown on British soil, on a 25acre plot of land, in the beautiful countryside of summerset. Its a small family run buisness with a passion for all things green.
90% of all our eletricty comes from the 5 acre solar farm we had installed so our costs come down, and these savings directly impact our prices for you. Because we are the cheapest bulb and cut flow specialist in the UK.
