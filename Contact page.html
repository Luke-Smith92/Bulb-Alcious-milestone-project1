Why choose us?

All our bulbs and flowers are grown on British soil, on a 25acre plot of land, in the beautiful countryside of summerset. Its a small family run buisness with a passion for all things green. We have a wide range of cut flowers, from roses to tulips, and everything in between. We also offer a subscription service for regular deliveries.

Our flowers are hand-picked and arranged by our expert florists, ensuring that you receive the freshest and most beautiful blooms.

We are proud to say that we are the cheapest bulb and cut flower specialist in the UK. We have a wide range of bulbs and flowers to choose from, all at competitive prices. We also offer free delivery on orders over £50.

We are committed to sustainability and reducing our carbon footprint. We use eco-friendly packaging and all our bulbs and flowers are grown using sustainable farming practices. We also have a solar farm on site, which generates 90% of our electricity. This means that we can keep our costs down and pass the savings on to you.
