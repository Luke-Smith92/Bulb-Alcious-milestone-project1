Welcome All

Welcome to Bulb-Alicious, a family-run flower business dedicated to everything that grows from the ground. Our passion lies mainly with bulbs, but we also offer beautifully arranged cut flower boxes for a wide range of occasions.

From the cheerful spring daffodils to the elegant summer lilies and the deep blue hyacinths of autumn, we celebrate flowers throughout the year. Our flowers are grown and prepared with care, ensuring quality and freshness in every arrangement.

This website has been created to showcase what we offer, provide information about our business, and allow users to explore our range with ease. It is designed to give visitors a clear understanding of our products, our values, and the services we provide before visiting us in person.

Whether you are planning a wedding, celebrating an anniversary, or simply looking to brighten your home, Bulb-Alicious aims to supply flowers that bring happiness all year round.
