Contact & About Us

Welcome to Bulb-Alicious! We are a small family-run flower business passionate about everything that grows from the ground. Our bulbs and cut flowers are grown with love in the beautiful British countryside, on a 25-acre plot. We believe flowers should bring joy to your home and garden, and we take pride in ensuring every bloom is perfect.

Visitors are always welcome! Feel free to drop by our flower center to see our bulbs, seasonal flowers, and arrangements in person. Whether you're looking for spring daffodils, summer lilies, or autumn hyacinths, we have something to brighten your day. Our friendly team loves sharing tips and helping you choose the perfect plants for your garden.

We also offer advice on planting, caring for bulbs, and creating beautiful cut flower arrangements. Come visit us and experience the joy of flowers up close — it's more than just shopping, it's an experience!
